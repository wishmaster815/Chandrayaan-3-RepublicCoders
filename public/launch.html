Chandrayaan-3: India's Stellar Journey to Unravel the Moon's Mysteries

Chandrayaan-3, India's third lunar mission, successfully launched on 14 July 2023. It aims to explore the Moon's south polar region, building on the successes of Chandrayaan-1 and Chandrayaan-2. The mission includes a lunar lander and rover to conduct scientific experiments, advancing India's space exploration capabilities.
